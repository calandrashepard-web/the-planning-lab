# The Planning Lab

A merchandise planning simulator built as a portfolio piece.

Step into the role of a Director of Planning. Choose a brand — either Ellery St. (fashion apparel) or Basecamp Nutrition (CPG) — and navigate a full seasonal planning cycle across 5 rounds:

1. **Pre-Season Planning** — set annual targets, sell-through, and growth
2. **Category Investment Mix** — allocate the buy across categories
3. **Newness & Productivity** — balance assortment depth and new introductions
4. **In-Season React** — make real-time chase and cancel decisions
5. **Promotions & Markdowns** — optimize end-of-season pricing

At the end, see where you rank — from Associate Planner to VP of Planning.
